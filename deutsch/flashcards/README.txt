HOW TO USE THESE FLASHCARDS:

You will need to download Anki, which is a free open-source flash card program for your PC/Mac. (Not sponsored I just like it!)

Download Anki from their official website HERE:

https://apps.ankiweb.net/

#
#
#

Once you've followed the instructions in the installation wizard, the first thing you'll want to do is go to the buttons at the bottom of the Anki window and click "Import File" on the right.

Then, navigate to the flashcard packs I've included in this download and import them.

Now you should be ready to study!
